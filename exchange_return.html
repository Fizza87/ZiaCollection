Exchange & Return Policy

Our Policy

We accept exchanges and returns within 14 days of purchase. Products must be in original condition with tags and packaging intact.

How to Request an Exchange or Return:

  1. Go to your Orders page.
  2. Select the item you want to exchange or return.
  3. Click on "Request Exchange/Return".
  4. Fill out the form with your reason.
  5. Follow the instructions for shipping your item back.

Refunds:

Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. Original payment method will be used for the refund.
